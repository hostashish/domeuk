Pushkar Singh Dhami

Chief minister of Uttarakhand

We are committed to provide the best health services in our state. A good health service is not possible without the help of well trained, skilled, committed and energetic human resources along with the good infrastructure. Therefore, it is our duty to provide well skilled and quality education in medical, Dental, Paramedical, Nursing courses, so that essential services can be given to people in the state as well as other part of the country. The educational and academic curriculum of medical, dental and nursing is well maintained on the pattern of Medical/Dental/Nursing Council’s of India.
